Our Application Features

Cardiac Monitoring

Through Cexme - Health Smartwatch, check your ECG minimum once a week at 07.00 to 08.00 am. Combining your ECG data & Medical Record, Doctor will update your Risk Score for HAS-BLED and CHA2DS2- VASc

Early Warning System

Beside ECG, Cexme - Health Smartwatch provide automatic vital signs update your Early Warning Score every hour: Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, Temperature, Blood Oxygen, and Respiratory Rate. If High Risk is detected, please contact your doctor immediately

My Pacemaker

Pacemaker is your vital device. Please update your Pacemaker device via your doctor, and update your battery status via Cexme - Health Smartwatch. If anything happen without your regular doctor, just show this updated Pacemaker status.

IoMT - Health Tracker

To provide easy and automatic health data update, Harmoni is connected to health tracker: Cexme - Health Smartwatch. Soon, Harmoni will be connected to other Smartwatch, Body Analyzer Scale, Blood Glucose, etc.

Teleconsultation

In case you are not feeling well, or receiving High Risk Status, but you can not go to hospital, just do Teleconsultation to your doctor via application. Please relax, upon your approval, your doctor can read your daily medical record in this application

Medical Record

As a Mobile Medical Record that receive continuous Vital Sign & ECG from Cexme - Health Smartwatch, data from your Pacemaker, and the Diagnosis after Teleconsultation, you can add your own Medical Record such as Rontgen, Medicine, Labtest, Genome, USG, and Personal Note.

Health Screening

This feature is for checking your potential Heart Disease or Stroke. You can check via Questionnaire for your previous health data. But for current data, you can check via Cexme - Health Smartwatch. Just Fill in your sleeping time, and wear the smartwatch for 7x24 hours, then you can get the analysis.

Sport Monitoring

To maintain your health and to avoid sudden heart attack during sport activity, please use your sport monitoring to control your heart rate. There are 2 lines, green & red. You are on sport mode when you reach green line. But, for safety, please take a rest if you pass the red line.
